## Overview

Cicero is a decentralised borrowing and lending protocol that enables uncollateralized lending for both on-chain and off-chain borrowers. The protocol is operated by 3 main participants:

<CardGroup cols="3">
  <Card title="Lenders">
    Cicero liquidity is provided by lenders who deposit their stablecoins into pools.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Borrowers">
    Entities originating the loans on Cicero are called borrowers.&#x20;
  </Card>
  <Card title="Governor">
    Governor controls the protocol operations through Cicero DAO.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
